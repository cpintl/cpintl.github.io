# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting cultural differences and perspectives

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination or exclusion based on protected characteristics

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. Document what happened, when, and who was involved
2. Email [ariful@cpi-ypsa.org](mailto:ariful@cpi-ypsa.org)
3. Include specific details and context

We will not tolerate retaliation against reporters acting in good faith.

### Investigation Process

- All reports are reviewed within 72 hours
- Investigations are conducted confidentially
- Both parties may be interviewed
- Decisions are final

### Possible Consequences

Enforcement actions may include:

1. **Warning** — Private discussion about the behavior
2. **Temporary Ban** — Removal from project for 30-90 days
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal from project
4. **Escalation** — Legal action in cases of serious harm

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, email, forums, etc.)
- Official community events and conferences
- Interactions that represent the project or community
